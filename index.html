About Titan Times

Titan Times is a student-run online publication serving University High School in Orange City, Florida, home of the Titans.

Our goals: highlight student achievement, provide reliable information, support campus involvement, and connect Titans with the Volusia community.

Titan Times is independent student media and is not an official press release channel of Volusia County Schools.
